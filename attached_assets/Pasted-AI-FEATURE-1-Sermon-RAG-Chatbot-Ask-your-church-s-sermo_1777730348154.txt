PROMPT 4 — Drift Risk Predictor
Add a Drift Risk Predictor to the Ghost Member Detector in ChurchOS.

The app already has members at GET /api/members with fields:
id, churchName, fullName, phone, email, cellGroup, cellLeaderEmail,
joinDate, lastAttendance, consecutiveMisses, status, createdAt

STEP 1 — GET /api/members/drift-risk?churchName=X
Analyses all members for that church and returns drift risk scores.

For each member, calculate a DRIFT SCORE (0-100) based on:

Factor 1: Attendance trajectory (40 points max)
- consecutiveMisses == 0 AND lastAttendance within 7 days: 0 points (no risk)
- consecutiveMisses == 0 AND lastAttendance 8-14 days ago: 10 points
- consecutiveMisses == 1: 25 points
- consecutiveMisses == 2: status already 'atRisk', still add 40 points
- consecutiveMisses >= 4: already ghost

Factor 2: Days since joining vs engagement (30 points max)
- Joined more than 90 days ago AND consecutiveMisses >= 1: add 15 points
- Joined more than 180 days ago AND consecutiveMisses >= 1: add 30 points
- (Long-standing members who start missing are higher risk than new members)

Factor 3: Giving correlation (30 points max)
- Cross-reference with giving table: find records where donorPhone matches member phone
- If member has never given: add 10 points (lower engagement signal)
- If member gave previously but no giving in last 30 days: add 20 points
- If member gave previously but no giving in last 60 days: add 30 points

DRIFT LEVEL classification:
- Score 0-20: "Healthy" (no action needed)
- Score 21-40: "Watch" (monitor, no alert yet)
- Score 41-60: "Drift Risk" 🟡 (trigger a proactive check-in)
- Score 61-100: "High Risk" 🔴 (immediate pastoral outreach)

For all members with driftLevel "Drift Risk" or "High Risk":
- Generate a personalised AI check-in message using OpenAI (gpt-4o-mini):
  
  Prompt: "Write a warm, casual WhatsApp message (max 2 sentences) 
  from a cell leader checking in on a church member named [firstName] 
  who has been attending [churchName] for [X months]. The message should 
  feel personal and caring, not robotic. No mention of attendance or giving. 
  Sign it as 'Your [cellGroup] family'. Nigerian-English tone is fine."
  
  Store this as suggestedMessage in the response.
  If no OpenAI key: suggestedMessage = "Hi [firstName]! Just thinking about 
  you today and wanted to check in. How are you doing? 🙏 — [cellGroup] family"

Return JSON:
{
  totalMembers: N,
  healthy: [...members],
  watch: [...members],
  driftRisk: [...members with driftScore and suggestedMessage],
  highRisk: [...members with driftScore and suggestedMessage]
}

STEP 2 — Notifications
For each "High Risk" member where no drift-risk notification exists yet:
Insert into notifications table:
  type: "drift-risk"
  recipient: member.cellLeaderEmail
  subject: "Early warning: [fullName] — [cellGroup]"
  body: "Our system has detected early drift signals for [fullName] in [cellGroup].
  They are not yet officially absent but engagement indicators suggest they 
  may be drifting. Suggested message to send them this week:
  
  '[suggestedMessage]'
  
  Sending this message now takes 30 seconds and could retain a member. 
  Last attendance: [lastAttendance] | Score: [driftScore]/100"

STEP 3 — Frontend: Drift Risk Panel on Members Dashboard
On the existing Members/Dashboard page, add a new tab: "Early Warning"
